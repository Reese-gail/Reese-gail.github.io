    Today's Plan:

      Purpose:

      Welcome to my student friendly, interactive planner. As I said on the previous pages, the most effective way to stay organized with dates and tasks is by using a planner. This is helpful to use for school work and other reminders. Personally, I write everything I need to remember down, so this gives you one place to keep it all together. I hope this website has given you all the background information and resources you need to improve your time managemnt skills, now it is time to put that knowledge to the test by using this planner. Good luck and happy time managing!

      How to Use:

      This interactive planner is designed to help you organzie your thoughts efficiently, but also quick and easy. This allows you to have one place to see all your tasks. Simply type the title of the task, for example "math homework", into the task bar. Then type the exact time and AM or PM into the time bar. Then, when you hit "add task", it will appear below in the form of a bulleted list. Feel free to add as many or as few tasks as you need, this is your space now!
